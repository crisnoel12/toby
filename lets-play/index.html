Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

Rules

This game is a game of matchup. Each round, players pick their choice from the following: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard or Spock. If both players pick the same choice, then the result will end up as a tie. Each choice beats and loses to two specific choices.

  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Rock crushes Scissors

  • Paper covers Rock
  • Paper disproves Spock

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard

  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Lizard eats Paper

  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
